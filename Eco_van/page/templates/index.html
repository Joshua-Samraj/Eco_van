Our Mission

Living Hope Charitable Trust Tirunelveli is dedicated to uplifting the lives of the most vulnerable: the orphaned, elderly, and mentally ill individuals living on the streets. Through our eco-van project, we aim to deliver food, provide medical aid, and rescue those in need—while respecting human dignity and promoting sustainability.

Eco-Van Services

  • Rescue of orphaned, elderly, and mentally ill individuals
  • Dignified hospital transport and medical assistance
  • Daily food distribution to over 300 people monthly
  • Monthly "Fight Against Hunger" initiatives for 400+ individuals
  • Mobile medical camps, awareness campaigns, cancer medicine support
  • Mini ambulance services and emergency care
  • Family reunification and long-term welfare integration

Our Impact

Every month, our volunteers serve over 300 meals, assist in hospital transports, and reunite families. The ECCO van will multiply our reach across districts, including rural and urban Tirunelveli, and extend into Tamil Nadu and Puducherry.
